### 🧬 AI-powered Variant Analysis 💻
❓ The challenge in modern genomics is no longer just generating data—it’s interpreting it. 

💡 Whether you are looking for a deep theoretical dive or a hands-on tool for your next project, here is a curated toolkit for AI-powered variant analysis: prioritizing, interpreting and communicating variant findings.

1. #### 📚 Recent review papers (great overviews):
    1. Artificial intelligence in variant calling: a review 
        - Ref: [Frontiers in Bioinformatics 2025](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40337525/) 
    2. Harnessing artificial intelligence for genomic variant prediction: advances, challenges, and future directions 
        - Ref: [Gigascience 2026](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41518203/)

2. #### 🛠️ Freeely available web-based tools: 
    1. HumanBase 
        - An interactive AI platform for human biology that uses tissue‑specific gene networks to predict functional impacts
        - 🌐 [humanbase.io](https://humanbase.io/)
        - Ref: [Nature Methods 2026](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41535395/)
    2. FAVOR-GPT
        - A generative AI interface to FAVOR (Functional Annotation of genomic Variants Online Resources)
        - 🌐 [favor.genohub.org](https://favor.genohub.org) 
        - Ref: [Bioinformatics Advances 2024](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39387060/)
    3. VarChat 
        - A generative AI assistant for variant literature and summary
        - 🌐 [varchat.engenome.com](https://varchat.engenome.com/) 
        - Ref: [Bioinformatics 2024](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38579245)

3. #### 💻 Together, these tools span the core steps of an AI-powered variant analysis workflow:
- Variant calling 
- Functional prediction and annotation
- Gene network and pathway context
- Literature retrieval and interpretation
